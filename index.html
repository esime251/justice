Why is justice important?

Justice is important in making sure that any and everybody is able to guard their rights, no matter their gender, race, or their financial situation. Justice also ensures that every individual regardless of their societal status, gets the punishment they desreve.

Why is reconciliation important?

Reconciliation is important in mending relationships with the people you are fighting with and rebuilding trust with your friends and family. It heps you to further develop your ability to forgive others and berings peace between you and the opposing party.

How do they go hand in hand?

At times, during sentencing, the criminal is ordered to pay a fine for funeral expenses if they have killed someone. This tells us that justice when served can later lead to lead to reconciliation, when the victim is given compensation from the criminal over the loss of their loved one
