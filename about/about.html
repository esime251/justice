Why is justice important? Why is reconciliation a priority? How do they go hand in hand and interconnect in order to help individuals? If you have been wondering this for some time, this website aims to answer all your questions and give you an understanding of justice, reconciliation and their relationship.

What is Reconciliation?

When two or more individuals are brought together after an arguement or disagreement is what we call reconciliation. Reconciliation helps to mend relationships, rebuild trust and adresses issues. An example of this is two friends who are going through a disagreement deciding to sit down, adress the issue and apologize for their actions, leading to peace and better communication.

Why is Justice important?

Justice is the act of sanctioning someone who has done wrong and giving them what he or she deserves. For example, when you steal, the penalty for that will be jail time or a fine. This is known as justice.
